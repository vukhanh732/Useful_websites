# Useful Free Websites

A curated list of free websites worth bookmarking.

## Design & Dev Tools

- **[Uiverse](https://uiverse.io/)** — Community-made library of free, open-source CSS/Tailwind UI components (buttons, cards, inputs, animations) you can copy straight into your projects.

## Film & Video Reference

- **[Eyecandy](https://eyecannndy.com/)** — Visual technique library for filmmakers, with free tutorials and inspirational content covering camera and editing craft.
- **[Shotdeck](https://shotdeck.com/)** — Huge searchable library of HD film, TV, and commercial screenshots, hand-tagged by cinematography details (lighting, framing, lens, color, mood) for visual reference.
- **[Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/)** — Free stock video clips, music tracks, and templates, no watermark or attribution required.

## Brain Training

- **[Memory League](https://memoryleague.com/)** — Online memory training and competition platform with games for memorizing images, names, words, numbers, and cards.
- **[Drill Your Skill](https://drillyourskill.com/)** — Free mental math trainer covering addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and Flash Anzan-style chain math, with real-time races and survival mode.

## Entertainment Discovery

- **[Reely](https://www.reely.space/)** — Site for discovering trending, top-rated, and popular movies and TV shows and tracking what you watch.
